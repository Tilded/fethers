ðŸª“Drago the Barbarian - Enhancements & Progression

Unleash your primal fury and become an unstoppable force!

Tier 1 Enhancements (Levels 1-5)

Thick Skin

+1 AC permanently. Your skin is tough as leather!

Berserker's Strength

You deal +1 additional damage when your HP is below half. You get stronger when hurt!

Wild Sprint

Your Speed becomes 7 squares. Close distances quickly!

Intimidating Roar

Once per adventure, all enemies within 3 squares of you can't attack you next round. Make them tremble!

Tough as Nails

+3 HP instead of +1 this level. You're super tough!

Tier 2 Enhancements (Levels 6-10)

Iron Hide

+1 AC permanently (requires Thick Skin, for a total of +2 AC outdoors, +1 AC indoors). Your skin becomes as tough as leather armor!

Frenzy

When you score a critical hit, you can immediately make another attack. More damage from lucky strikes!

Leap Attack

You can jump up to 4 squares and attack with +2 damage. Bypass obstacles and surprise enemies!

Unstoppable Rage

Once per adventure, you can ignore all negative conditions (like being knocked down or shackled) for 3 rounds. Nothing can hold you back!

Cleaving Strikes

Your attacks can hit two adjacent enemies (roll separately for each). Effective against groups!

Tier 3 Enhancements (Levels 11-15)

Stone Skin

You ignore the first 2 points of damage taken in each combat encounter. Incredibly difficult to wear down!

Whirlwind

Once per adventure, you can attack all enemies within 2 squares. A devastating area attack!

Primal Speed

Once per adventure, you can move twice in one turn. Incredible repositioning or chasing!

Terrifying Presence

Enemies within 4 squares of you get -2 to all their attack rolls and skill checks. Your presence demoralizes foes!

Endless Stamina

When below half HP, you heal 1 HP at the start of each of your turns. Hard to defeat once wounded!

Tier 4 Enhancements (Levels 16-19)

Legendary Toughness

Your maximum HP increases by +5 permanently. The most durable hero!

Avatar of Rage

Once per adventure, you can double all damage you deal for 3 rounds. A game-changing burst of destruction!

Earthquake Stomp

Once per adventure, you can knock down all enemies within 4 squares. Disrupt enemy formations!

Immortal Warrior

The first time you would be defeated each adventure, you instead return with 1 HP. You simply refuse to stay down!

Level 20 Master Enhancement: Avatar of Endless Rage

Once per adventure, Drago can enter ULTIMATE RAGE MODE for an entire combat. During Ultimate Rage: Drago gets 3 attacks per turn, deals +5 damage on all attacks, and cannot be reduced below 1 HP (literally too angry to fall). When Ultimate Rage ends, Drago explodes with primal energy - all enemies within 4 squares take 3 damage and are knocked down. The sheer intensity causes the ground to crack wherever Drago walks, leaving permanent marks of their legendary fury.

Milestone Equipment

Your gear gets stronger as you become more legendary!

Level 5: Heroic Gear
All your weapons deal +1 damage permanently. All your armor grants +1 AC permanently.
Level 10: Champion's Arsenal
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +2). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +2).
Level 15: Legendary Equipment
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +3). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +3).
Level 20: Artifact Mastery
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +4). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +4).

Your DM will also give you special magical items at these levels!
