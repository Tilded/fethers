üõ°Ô∏èSchmitt the Fighter - Enhancements & Progression

Master your combat prowess and lead your allies!

Tier 1 Enhancements (Levels 1-5)

Shield Master

+1 AC permanently. You're even harder to hit!

Weapon Expert

+1 attack bonus permanently with your favored weapon (e.g., Longsword). Your strikes are more accurate!

Tough Fighter

+2 HP instead of +1 this level. You're an absolute tank!

Battle Surge

Once per adventure, you can take an extra attack immediately after your normal attack. Unleash a powerful combo!

Defensive Stance

Once per adventure, you can gain +3 AC until your next turn. Brace for impact!

Tier 2 Enhancements (Levels 6-10)

Advanced Shield

+2 AC when using a shield (requires Shield Master). This stacks for formidable defense!

Weapon Mastery

Critical hits on a roll of 19 or 20 (requires Weapon Expert). Your hits are truly devastating!

Combat Reflexes

Once per round, you can make an attack against an enemy who moves adjacent to you. Punish foes who try to get past you!

Guardian

Any adjacent allies (heroes or friendly creatures) gain +1 AC. You become a living shield!

Charge Attack

Your Charge special power now deals +1 additional damage (total +3 damage). Your signature move becomes even more impactful!

Tier 3 Enhancements (Levels 11-15)

Shield Slam

Use your shield as a weapon (1 damage, +1 attack bonus), and knock enemies down (they lose their next move action) on a hit. Control the battlefield with your shield!

Weapon Hurricane

Once per adventure, you can attack all enemies adjacent to you. A powerful crowd-clearing ability!

Armor Expertise

+1 AC permanently, and you ignore the first point of damage taken in each combat encounter. Incredibly resilient!

Battle Leader

All allies get +1 to their next attack after you hit an enemy. Inspire your team to strike harder!

Unstoppable

Once per adventure, you can ignore all damage for one full round. An unbreakable wall!

Tier 4 Enhancements (Levels 16-19)

Master Guardian

Allies within 2 squares of you get +2 AC. Your protective aura expands and strengthens!

Legendary Weapon

Your favored weapon deals an additional +1 damage permanently (total +2 from Weapon Expert + Legendary Weapon). An extension of your heroic will!

Perfect Defense

Once per adventure, you can become unhittable for one round. No attack can touch you!

Inspiring Strike

Critical hits from you give all allies +2 to their next attack. Your triumphs elevate the entire party!

Level 20 Master Enhancement: Legendary Guardian

Schmitt becomes UNDEFEATABLE for one full combat per adventure - all attacks automatically miss him, and he heals 2 HP at the start of each turn. He can protect the entire party - any damage dealt to allies within 3 squares gets redirected to him instead (and he still takes no damage during his undefeatable turn!). His presence is so inspiring that all allies get +3 to everything (attacks, AC, damage) while he's conscious.

Milestone Equipment

Your gear gets stronger as you become more legendary!

Level 5: Heroic Gear
All your weapons deal +1 damage permanently. All your armor grants +1 AC permanently.
Level 10: Champion's Arsenal
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +2). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +2).
Level 15: Legendary Equipment
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +3). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +3).
Level 20: Artifact Mastery
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +4). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +4).

Your DM will also give you special magical items at these levels!
