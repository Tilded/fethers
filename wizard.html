Out of Combat Actions

When you're not fighting, try these ideas if you're stuck!

Explore: Look around carefully for clues, hidden paths, or interesting things. (Roll 10+ for normal, 15+ for hard)
Search: Actively look for hidden items, traps, or secret doors. (Roll 10+ for normal, 15+ for hard)

✨Mosley the Wizard - Enhancements & Progression

Master of arcane power and reality itself!

Tier 1 Enhancements (Levels 1-5)

Shield Spell

Cast a magical shield that grants +3 AC until hit. Recharges after 3 rounds. Your magical defense!

Mystic Recovery

+1 to all your attack rolls permanently. Your magical aim becomes more precise!

Cantrip Master

Use minor magic effects at will (e.g., create light, make small sounds, change temperature, make objects float, conjure simple illusions). Get creative!

Spell Focus

+1 damage to all your magical attacks permanently. Your spells hit harder!

Magic Missile

Once per round, you can automatically hit one enemy for 1 damage (no attack roll needed). A reliable magical blast!

Tier 2 Enhancements (Levels 6-10)

Greater Shield

The Shield Spell now grants +4 AC (requires Shield Spell). Your magical defenses become even stronger!

Spell Penetration

Your spells ignore enemy resistances to magic (requires Mystic Recovery). No foe is truly immune to your power!

Utility Magic

You gain major convenience spells (e.g., fly for short distances, teleport short distance up to 3 squares, create more complex illusions). Enhance exploration and tactics!

Fireball

Once per adventure, you can deal 2 damage to all enemies in a 3x3 square area. A classic, devastating area-of-effect spell!

Counterspell

Once per adventure, you can stop an enemy's magic attack or special power. Save the party from dangerous effects!

Tier 3 Enhancements (Levels 11-15)

Master of Elements

+2 AC permanently, and your spells can be visually manifested as fire, ice, or lightning. More resilient and visually impressive!

Spell Mastery

You can cast any Tier 2 enhancement spell twice per adventure. Use your most useful spells more often!

Dimension Door

Once per adventure, you can teleport anywhere you can see. Incredible mobility and escape options!

Lightning Bolt

Once per adventure, you can deal 3 damage to all enemies in a straight line (up to 6 squares long). A powerful, piercing attack!

Time Dilation

Once per adventure, all allies (including you) get an extra turn. Completely turn the tide of battle!

Tier 4 Enhancements (Levels 16-19)

Archmage

All of your spells deal +2 damage, and you gain +4 AC permanently. A walking force of magical destruction and surprisingly tough!

Meteor

Once per adventure, you can call down a meteor, dealing 4 damage to all enemies in a 5x5 square area. An apocalyptic, battle-ending spell!

Wish

Once per adventure, you can recreate any enhancement from any class (even other heroes!). The ultimate versatile power!

Reality Warp

Once per adventure, you can change any die roll result (your own or an enemy's) to any number you choose. Literally rewrite fate!

Level 20 Master Enhancement: Master of Reality

Once per adventure, Mosley can cast THE ULTIMATE SPELL - they describe any magical effect they want, and it happens (within reason - no "I win instantly"). Examples: Turn all enemies into harmless rabbits for 3 rounds, create a massive dragon made of pure magic energy to fight for them, open a portal to anywhere in the world, stop time for everyone except the heroes. Mosley becomes immune to all damage from spells and magical effects permanently. Their magic is so powerful that reality bends around them - once per turn, they can reroll ANY die roll made by anyone (including enemies) and choose which result to use.

Milestone Equipment

Your gear gets stronger as you become more legendary!

Level 5: Heroic Gear
All your weapons deal +1 damage permanently. All your armor grants +1 AC permanently.
Level 10: Champion's Arsenal
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +2). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +2).
Level 15: Legendary Equipment
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +3). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +3).
Level 20: Artifact Mastery
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +4). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +4).

Your DM will also give you special magical items at these levels!
