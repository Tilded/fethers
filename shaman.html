ðŸŒ¿Zug the Shaman - Enhancements & Progression

Healer and master of spirits!

Tier 1 Enhancements (Levels 1-5)

Nature's Ally

+1 AC permanently when you are outdoors. Nature protects you!

Ancestor's Wisdom

+1 to all your attack rolls permanently. The spirits guide your aim!

Healing Spirits

Once per adventure, you can heal 2 HP to any ally. A vital healing touch!

Animal Friend

Once per adventure, you can summon a helpful animal (like a loyal wolf!) for one encounter. It has 3 HP, 10 AC, and attacks for 1 damage.

Spirit Shield

Once per adventure, you can gain +2 AC for one round. A quick burst of defense!

Tier 2 Enhancements (Levels 6-10)

Bark Skin

+1 AC permanently anywhere (requires Nature's Ally, for a total of +2 AC outdoors, +1 AC indoors). Your skin becomes tougher, like tree bark!

Spirit Guide

Once per round, you can reroll any attack roll (your own or an ally's) (requires Ancestor's Wisdom). The spirits offer a second chance!

Greater Healing

Once per adventure, you can heal 4 HP to any ally (requires Healing Spirits). A more powerful healing touch!

Beast Form

Once per adventure, you can transform into an animal (e.g., a powerful bear) for one encounter. Gain the animal's HP, AC, Speed, and attack for versatile combat!

Spectral Army

Once per adventure, you can summon 3 spirit warriors for one fight (1 HP, 10 AC, 1 damage each). Overwhelm enemies with numbers!

Tier 3 Enhancements (Levels 11-15)

One with Nature

When outdoors, you heal 1 HP at the start of each of your turns. The natural world constantly rejuvenates you!

Ancestral Champion

Once per adventure, you can get +5 to all your attack rolls and skill checks for 3 rounds. The power of your ancestors flows through you!

Mass Healing

Once per adventure, you can heal all allies within 3 squares for 3 HP. A powerful group heal for tough fights!

Elemental Control

You can command fire, water, earth, or air in dramatic ways (e.g., create a wall of fire, summon a geyser). Powerful utility spells!

Spirit Walker

Once per adventure, you can become incorporeal for one round (move through objects, ignore physical attacks). Untouchable and able to pass through barriers!

Tier 4 Enhancements (Levels 16-19)

Avatar of Nature

Once per adventure, you can gain flight, +3 AC, and +3 damage to your attacks for 5 rounds. A powerful, soaring force of nature!

Master Healer

Once per adventure, you can heal any ally to full HP. Turn the tide of the most desperate battles!

Planar Ally

Once per adventure, you can summon a powerful spirit (e.g., an elemental, a nature giant) that fights for the entire encounter. A formidable companion!

Reincarnation

Once per adventure, you can bring a defeated ally back to life with half HP. Even death is not the end when you are present!

Level 20 Master Enhancement: World-Shaper

Once per adventure, Zug can completely reshape the battlefield - create walls, chasms, rivers, or even floating platforms. They can summon a Legendary Spirit Beast (like a giant ethereal wolf or cosmic eagle) that fights alongside the party for the entire adventure. Zug gains command over weather - can call down lightning (3 damage to any enemy), create healing rain (+2 HP to all allies per turn), or summon protective mists (+2 AC to everyone). Most amazingly, Zug can bring back ANY defeated ally as a spirit warrior - they return with full HP and gain ghostly powers (can fly and phase through walls).

Milestone Equipment

Your gear gets stronger as you become more legendary!

Level 5: Heroic Gear
All your weapons deal +1 damage permanently. All your armor grants +1 AC permanently.
Level 10: Champion's Arsenal
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +2). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +2).
Level 15: Legendary Equipment
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +3). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +3).
Level 20: Artifact Mastery
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +4). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +4).

Your DM will also give you special magical items at these levels!
