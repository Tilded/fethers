Core Conflict

This campaign revolves around the insidious resurgence of the **Cult of the Ouroboros**, a malevolent necromantic cult led by the ancient **Lich Lord Kaelen**. The cult's goal is to unravel the natural cycle of life and death, transforming the world into a realm of eternal undeath where their dark patron can manifest. The heroes will uncover this conspiracy piece by piece, as each adventure reveals another facet of Kaelen's grand plan.

Opposing this decay is **Somnia the Dreamweaver**, a deity of growth and potential who aids the heroes through dreams and divine gifts, acting as a crucial counterbalance to the cult's dark influence.

Heroes of Hesiod Rules

This campaign is adapted for the "Heroes of Hesiod" system for younger players, focusing on simple turns, fast combat, and clear rewards.

  • **Progression:** Levels are gained after a set number of adventures, not XP.
  • **Combat:** Monsters are simplified into roles (Minion, Elite, etc.) with low HP for faster encounters.
  • **Rewards:** Players get tangible rewards like magic items (often from Somnia) after key victories.

Top DM Tips

  • **Describe Vividly:** Use all five senses to immerse your players.
  • **Encourage Roleplaying:** Ask "What do you do?" and reward creativity.
  • **Be Flexible:** Player choices drive the story. Improvise and adapt.
  • **Rule of Cool:** If an idea is awesome, find a way to make it work.
  • **Have Fun:** Your enthusiasm is contagious!
