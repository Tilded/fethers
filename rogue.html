Out of Combat Actions

When you're not fighting, try these ideas if you're stuck!

Explore: Look around carefully for clues, hidden paths, or interesting things. (Roll 10+ for normal, 15+ for hard)
Search: Actively look for hidden items, traps, or secret doors. (Roll 10+ for normal, 15+ for hard)

Pick Lock: Open locked doors or chests with your tools. (Roll 10+ for simple, 15+ for complex)

ðŸ”ªMileena the Rogue - Enhancements & Progression

Master of stealth and cunning, striking from the shadows.

Tier 1 Enhancements (Levels 1-5)

Dodge

+1 AC permanently. You're even more elusive!

Precise Strike

+1 damage permanently when you have advantage on an attack (like from Sneak Attack). Make every hit count!

Shadow Step

Once per adventure, you can teleport up to 6 squares. Great for getting into position or escaping danger!

Lucky

Once per adventure, you can reroll any die (your own or an enemy's). Turn a miss into a hit!

Fleet Footed

Your Speed becomes 8 squares. You're incredibly fast!

Tier 2 Enhancements (Levels 6-10)

Evasion

You gain +2 AC against ranged attacks (requires Dodge). Arrows and magic bolts find it even harder to hit you!

Assassinate

You deal +2 damage against surprised enemies (requires Precise Strike). Incredibly deadly opening attacks!

Vanish

Once per adventure, you can become invisible until you attack (requires Shadow Step). Perfect for surprise attacks or escapes!

Master Thief

You automatically succeed on simple skill checks (e.g., picking a simple lock, finding a hidden button). No secret can hide!

Acrobatic Fighter

You can move through enemy squares freely without provoking attacks. Weave through the battlefield with ease!

Tier 3 Enhancements (Levels 11-15)

Untouchable

Once per adventure, you can avoid all damage for one full round. You become a ghost, impossible to harm!

Death Strike

Once per adventure, your next attack is an automatic critical hit. A guaranteed devastating blow!

Shadow Clone

Once per adventure, you can create a duplicate that fights for 3 rounds (1 HP, 10 AC, 1 damage attack). A perfect distraction or extra attacker!

Thousand Cuts

Once per adventure, you can make 3 attacks instead of 1. A rapid flurry of blows!

Master Scout

You can see through walls and detect all traps automatically within a short range. No secret can hide from you!

Tier 4 Enhancements (Levels 16-19)

Legendary Dodge

You never take damage from area attacks (e.g., a dragon's breath). You are simply too quick to be caught!

Perfect Assassin

All attacks from surprise or when flanking an enemy are critical hits. The ultimate silent killer!

Shadow Master

You can teleport at will during combat (once per round, up to 6 squares). Appear and disappear like a whisper!

Time Stop

Once per adventure, you can take a full extra turn. Incredible tactical maneuvers!

Level 20 Master Enhancement: Shadow Clone Army

Once per adventure, Mileena can create FIVE perfect shadow clones that last for an entire combat. Each clone has Mileena's full stats but only 1 HP, and they all act on her turn. If any clone scores a critical hit, ALL clones (including the real Mileena) immediately teleport and make a bonus attack. When the clones disappear, the real Mileena becomes invisible until she attacks, and her next attack is automatically a critical hit.

Milestone Equipment

Your gear gets stronger as you become more legendary!

Level 5: Heroic Gear
All your weapons deal +1 damage permanently. All your armor grants +1 AC permanently.
Level 10: Champion's Arsenal
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +2). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +2).
Level 15: Legendary Equipment
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +3). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +3).
Level 20: Artifact Mastery
All your weapons deal an *additional* +1 damage (total +4). All your armor grants an *additional* +1 AC (total +4).

Your DM will also give you special magical items at these levels!
